## Top 5 Entry Points for Palimpsest

1. **Bonev & Cavalli (2016)** — structural hierarchy: territories→TADs→loops :: document→chapters→sentences
2. **Zerbino et al. (2020)** — annotation as scholarship: reference genome as a living edited text
3. **Mattick et al. (2023)** — most transcription is regulatory, not coding: most text is structure, not content
4. **Barabási & Oltvai (2004)** — gene networks as citation graphs with scale-free topology
5. **Senft & Macfarlan (2021)** — transposable elements as the genomic palimpsest proper
