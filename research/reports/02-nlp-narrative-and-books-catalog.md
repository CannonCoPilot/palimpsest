## Tier 1: Foundational Books (Must-Read)

### Macroanalysis: Digital Methods and Literary History
- **Author**: Matthew Lee Jockers | **Year**: 2013 | **Publisher**: University of Illinois Press
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 3d61fc0dfe793b2031c77e2ebb48698e (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Foundational text for digital literary studies. Jockers coined "macroanalysis" as the literary equivalent of distant reading. Covers topic modeling, stylometry, sentiment analysis at corpus scale. Created the Syuzhet R package for emotional arc extraction.

### Distant Reading
- **Author**: Franco Moretti | **Year**: 2013 | **Publisher**: Verso Books
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 2289aa1fcf8cfc2d69654cf2cf9099a3 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: The manifesto for computational literary studies. Moretti's Stanford Literary Lab pioneered quantitative approaches. Introduces "distant reading" as counterpoint to close reading — analyzing thousands of texts to reveal patterns invisible at the individual level.

### Close Reading with Computers: David Mitchell's Cloud Atlas
- **Author**: Martin Paul Eve | **Year**: 2019 | **Publisher**: Stanford University Press
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 0631e1fa85c6d0e794a5dd34a3d75a06 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates computational methods on a structurally complex novel. Cloud Atlas has 6 nested narratives — directly relevant to Palimpsest's structural analysis module.

### Mapping Texts: Computational Text Analysis for the Social Sciences
- **Authors**: Dustin S. Stoltz, Marshall A. Taylor | **Year**: 2023 | **Publisher**: Oxford University Press
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: a1d348a1e80894c02dd8760fef98bd75 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Modern systematic coverage of computational text analysis. Bridges NLP methods with interpretive frameworks.

### The Scales of (Computational) Literary Studies: Mueller's Scalable Reading
- **Author**: Benjamin Krautter | **Year**: 2023 | **Publisher**: de Gruyter
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 021c19b4be2f8e0f103232600adf5849 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Theory of "scalable reading" — moving fluidly between close and distant reading. Directly relevant to Palimpsest's multi-resolution approach.

### Visualize This: The FlowingData Guide (2nd Ed.)
- **Author**: Nathan Yau | **Year**: 2024 | **Publisher**: Wiley
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: ca20202082f7bdecd727de30317afc62 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Practical data visualization including text and narrative visualization. Reference for Palimpsest's visualization layer.

## Tier 2: Specialized References

### Making Sense of Narrative Text: Situation, Repetition, and Picturing
- **Author**: Michael J. Toolan | **Year**: 2016 | **Publisher**: Routledge
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 66dd9d5772b8de74103ef2f04b53c334 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Cognitive approach to narrative comprehension. Covers how readers construct mental models from text — relevant to computational modeling of narrative structure.

### The Comedy of Entropy: Humour, Narrative, Reading
- **Author**: Patrick O'Neill | **Year**: 1990 | **Publisher**: University of Toronto Press
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 5fb7127ef16039d835d4543ef5b2bbd1 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Information-theoretic approach to narrative. Connects Shannon entropy to literary structure — exactly the cross-domain bridge Palimpsest needs.

### Structural Analysis of Narrative Texts: Conference Papers
- **Editors**: Andrej Kodjak et al. | **Year**: 1980 | **Publisher**: Slavica Publishers
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 4e319fd9b20010fb6256c90993432e0c (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Early structuralist narratology. Historical foundation for computational approaches.

### Shakespeare's Queer Analytics: Distant Reading and Collaborative Intimacy
- **Authors**: Rodrigues D, Hope J, Witmore M, Magnusson L | **Year**: 2022 | **Publisher**: Bloomsbury
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: ad0b4296d4c9bc5d73e1386709587ea5 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Applied distant reading to a canonical literary corpus. Demonstrates methodological rigor in computational literary analysis.

### Blueprints for Text Analytics Using Python
- **Authors**: Albrecht J, Ramachandran S, Winkler C | **Year**: 2021 | **Publisher**: O'Reilly
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 2a74631bb6f3184c0f288fb668d3a3ca (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Practical NLP implementation. Text processing pipelines directly applicable to Palimpsest modules.

### Entropy and Information Theory (2nd Ed.)
- **Author**: Robert M. Gray | **Year**: 2011 | **Publisher**: Springer
- **Anna's Archive MD5**: 8839f2af0f911274fe40e68b3d1eacd7 (PDF)
- **Relevance**: Mathematical foundation for information-theoretic analysis of text. Covers entropy, mutual information, rate-distortion theory — tools for quantifying "surprise" and "information density" in narrative.

## Tier 3: Genome Browser Architecture (Based on articles retrieved from PubMed)

### Setting Up the JBrowse 2 Genome Browser
- **Authors**: Diesh C, Buels R, Stevens G, et al. (Holmes I)
- **Year**: 2024 | **Journal**: Current Protocols
- **DOI**: [10.1002/cpz1.1120](https://doi.org/10.1002/cpz1.1120) | **PMID**: 39126338
- **Relevance**: JBrowse 2 is the state-of-the-art modular genome browser. Its architecture (multi-track, zoomable, plugin-based) is the template for a "text browser" — a tool for navigating literary texts with annotation tracks (POS, NER, sentiment, dialog attribution) along the character-position axis.

### JBrowseR: An R Interface to JBrowse 2
- **Authors**: Hershberg EA, Stevens G, Diesh C, et al. (Holmes I)
- **Year**: 2021 | **Journal**: Bioinformatics
- **DOI**: [10.1093/bioinformatics/btab459](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btab459) | **PMID**: 34196689
- **Relevance**: R integration for JBrowse. Pattern for embedding genome browser visualizations in analytical notebooks.
